# IntelliScan: Global Feature Matrix

IntelliScan is a professional AI ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between physical networking and digital CRM workflows. It is divided into 4 primary functional pillars.

---

## 🚀 Pillar 1: Intelligent Data Ingestion
*   **AI Vision Engine**: Utilizes Google Gemini Pro Vision to extract over 15 data points (Name, Job, Phone, Socials) from a single photo.
*   **Contextual Inference**: The AI doesn't just read text; it "infers" missing information like the industry or seniority level based on the company name and title.
*   **Bulk Scanning**: Support for batch processing up to 50 cards simultaneously via ZIP upload or Kiosk Mode.
*   **Public Kiosk Mode**: A dedicated "Exhibition Mode" interface for iPads/tablets at physical events.

## 🏢 Pillar 2: Enterprise Workspace Management
*   **Shared Rolodex**: A unified database where team members can see each other's contacts (with privacy controls).
*   **Multi-Tenant RBAC**: Strict isolation between different companies (Organizations) using a single database.
*   **Custom CRM Mapping**: Enterprise admins can map IntelliScan variables to their internal Salesforce/HubSpot schemas.
*   **Org Chart Intelligence**: Visual representation of the sales team and their lead ownership hierarchy.

## 🤖 Pillar 3: AI-Driven Sales Acceleration
*   **AI Networking Coach**: A virtual advisor that analyzes your contact quality and suggests "Next Best Actions" (e.g., "Follow up with CEO of Tesla").
*   **Auto-Email Sequences**: AI drafts personalized emails immediately after a scan and schedules them for delivery.
*   **Opportunity Signals**: Detects when a lead is "hot" based on AI-analyzed engagement (click rates, open rates).
*   **Draft Hub**: A staging area for AI-authored follow-ups before they are sent to the prospect.

## 🛠️ Pillar 4: Platform Administration & Compliance
*   **Engine Performance (SuperAdmin)**: Real-time monitoring of AI latency, OCR accuracy rates, and API token health.
*   **GDPR / Privacy Center**: Tools to redact PII (Personally Identifiable Information) and manage data retention policies.
*   **System Incident Center**: A dashboard for tracking backend bugs, sync errors, and downtime alerts.
*   **Custom Model Tuning**: SuperAdmins can switch between AI providers (Gemini vs OpenAI) based on cost and performance.
