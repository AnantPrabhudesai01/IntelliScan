## 2) Document Formatting Requirements

Guideline summary (formatting rules are for the final print/PDF):

1. A4 page, 1 inch margins on all sides
2. Header: project title
3. Footer: page number + enrollment numbers
4. Font: Times New Roman
5. Main heading: 18 bold
6. Subheading: 16
7. Sub-subheading: 14
8. Paragraph: 12
9. Single line spacing
10. Double-sided printing
11. Roman page numbering before Chapter 1
12. Hardbound (black book) final submission

Repo note:

The repo provides content in markdown. Apply the above formatting when you convert to Word/PDF for final submission.
